## 2025-05-31 - MessageEvents/NotificationEvents Count Optimization
**Learning:** Calling `count($collector->getEvents()->getMessages())` on Symfony's Mailer and Notifier data collectors has O(N) lookup overhead because `getMessages()` instantiates a brand new array of mapped events on every call. In micro-benchmarks, calling `count($events->getEvents())` instead runs at O(1) in the underlying implementation and is vastly faster since it retrieves the pre-existing, raw internal array.
**Action:** Replaced `count($messageCollector->getEvents()->getMessages())` with `count($messageCollector->getEvents()->getEvents())` in `Codeception\Module\Symfony::debugMailerData` and `Codeception\Module\Symfony::debugNotifierData`.
